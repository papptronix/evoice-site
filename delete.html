Delete My Data

MyCar stores all data on your device — we do not operate any servers that hold your personal information. Here's how to remove everything.

1Delete data from within the app

The fastest way to remove your data:

  • Google Home: Settings → Disconnect Google Home (revokes OAuth tokens from your device)
  • API keys: Settings → Delete next to each key
  • Logs: ? → View Logs → Clear

2Uninstall the app

Uninstalling MyCar from your iPhone permanently deletes all app data — tokens, keys, and logs — from your device. Because we hold no server-side copy, uninstalling is a complete deletion.

3Revoke Google access independently

To revoke MyCar's access to your Google Account regardless of whether the app is installed, visit myaccount.google.com/permissions and remove MyCar from the list of connected apps.

4Request written confirmation

If you'd like written confirmation that no server-side data exists for you, send us an email. We'll respond within 30 days.
